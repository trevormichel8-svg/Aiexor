AI Startup Naming Trends in 2025

AI product names are shifting toward abstract, timeless, and short brand identities. In 2025, AI startups are choosing names that feel intelligent, neutral, and scalable. Aiexor fits the pattern used by fast-growing AI companies.

Top Naming Patterns

  • Short (4–7 letters)
  • Clean futuristic structure (XR, EX, OR)
  • Minimal vowel count
  • Abstract meaning over literal meaning
  • Works well as a verb (“Aiexor your workflow”)

Why This Trend Exists

AI tools often expand rapidly — from one feature to a suite of offerings. Founders choose names like Aiexor because they don’t restrict product evolution.
