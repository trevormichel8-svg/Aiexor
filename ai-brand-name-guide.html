The Complete AI Brand Name Guide (2025)

Choosing the right AI brand name can dramatically impact the trajectory of a startup. A strong name communicates trust, intelligence, speed, and future-proof potential.

What Makes a Strong AI Name?

  • Short (4–7 letters)
  • Globally pronounceable
  • Abstract yet futuristic
  • Easy to trademark and position
  • Phonetically “intelligent” sounding

Why Aiexor is an Ideal AI Brand

Aiexor uses high-value consonant architecture found in top AI brands. It evokes acceleration (“Xor”), intelligence (“AI”), and expansion (“Exor”).
