How to Choose the Perfect AI Brand Name

Naming your AI startup can influence investor trust, user adoption, and brand strength. The right name can position your product ahead of competitors immediately.

Key Elements of a Strong AI Startup Name

  • Short length — 4–7 letters are ideal
  • Tech-forward sound — endings like -or, -ex, -ix
  • Global pronounceability
  • .com availability
